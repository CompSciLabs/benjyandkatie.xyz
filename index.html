Location

Ceremony and Reception

Haven Green Baptist Church
Castlebar Road, Ealing, London, W5 2UP
Click on the pin on the map to the right for the location in google maps

By Car

There is no parking on the church site for guests,
There are carparks nearby to the Church which are:
Ealing Broadway Shopping Centre Carpark, Ealing Broadway, London, W5 5JY
Springbridge Road Carpark, Ealing, London, W5 2AB

By Public Transport

There is a tube station nearby called Ealing Broadway Station which is on the central line and the district line.
There are a multitude of buses which stop nearby to the church. Such buses are the E2, E8, E9, 297, 65
